FAQ

Common bird-proofing questions.

Are pigeon spikes humane?

Yes. Spikes are a landing deterrent, not a trap. They discourage pigeons from roosting without harming them.

Is balcony netting visible?

Netting is designed to be discreet and tidy. From normal viewing distance, it blends into the balcony far better than most people expect.

Can you install netting on high-rise balconies?

Yes, where access and building rules allow. Send photos and we can advise the safest approach.

Do you clean pigeon droppings before installation?

Yes. Balcony cleaning can be completed before netting or spikes so the protected area starts fresh.

Is the netting safe for cats and pets?

Pet and cat netting is selected and installed with pet safety in mind, especially on condo balconies.

How much does pigeon proofing cost?

Cost depends on size, access, material, and cleaning needs. Text photos for a free quote.
