How to Get a Faster Quote

Photos help us price the right solution.

The quickest way to start is to send clear photos of the balcony, ledge, roofline, sign, or building area where birds are landing. Include a wide photo that shows the whole space and a closer photo that shows droppings, nesting, ledges, or open gaps. If pets are involved, mention that too so we can consider pet-safe netting options.

We use those details to recommend the right service: balcony bird netting, pigeon spike installation, balcony cleaning, pet safety netting, or commercial bird control. If the project needs an in-person look, we will let you know. Either way, the quote process is meant to be clear, practical, and easy to start from your phone.

Helpful details to include

  • Your city or neighbourhood
  • Balcony or ledge photos
  • Approximate size of the area
  • Whether cleaning is needed
  • Pet, strata, or access requirements
