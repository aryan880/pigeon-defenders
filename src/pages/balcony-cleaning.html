Cleaning Before Prevention

A cleaner start for long-term protection.

Balcony cleaning is often the first step when pigeons have been nesting or roosting for a while. Droppings, feathers, nesting debris, and stained surfaces can make a balcony feel unusable even after the birds leave. Cleaning helps reset the space before a netting or spike system is installed.

We focus on practical balcony cleanup for condos, apartments, and strata properties across Metro Vancouver. If bird proofing is needed, cleaning and installation can be planned together so the balcony starts clean and has a better chance of staying that way. Photos are usually enough to begin a quote and understand the access, surface, and mess level.

After cleaning, we can point out where pigeons are likely to return and whether netting, spikes, or both will prevent the same issue from coming back. That makes the service more useful than a one-time cleanup alone.

Cleaning projects often include

  • Pigeon droppings and debris removal
  • Preparation before netting
  • Preparation before spike installation
