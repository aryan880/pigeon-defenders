Process

Photo review, cleanup plan, prevention advice.

The fastest way to begin is to text photos of the balcony. A wide photo shows size and access, while close-up photos show droppings, corners, ledges, furniture, or nesting material. We use those details to decide whether cleaning can be quoted from photos or whether the area needs a closer look.

Before work starts, we clarify the scope so you know what is included. Cleaning is planned around the surface, mess level, access, and whether a bird-proofing system will be installed afterward. Once the balcony is reset, we can recommend balcony bird netting, pigeon spikes, or a combined approach when the goal is to stop the same problem from returning.
